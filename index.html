Sophie Well is a deterministic public utility for patients, billers, coders, nurses, clinicians, EMS, and educators. Everything runs in the browser. No accounts, no telemetry, no AI. Sophie Well provides reference information and computed math from public data. Not medical, legal, or financial advice. It does not replace clinician judgment, institutional protocols, professional billing review, or legal counsel. It acts as a reference "cheat sheet" for healthcare workers.
